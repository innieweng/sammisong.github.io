What information is required?
  • Last year's (2013) Notice of Assessment/Reassessment and CRA correspondence
  • Copy of Last year's (2013) Income Tax return
  • Family members' information (Name, DOB, SIN, address, marital status)

What income should be reported?
  • Employment income T4
  • Other pensions and annuities T4A
  • Interest, Dividend T3, T5
  • Employment insurance benefits T4E
  • Old Age Security and CPP benefits T4A(OAS),T4A(P)
  • Universal Child Care Benefit RC62
  • Social assistance payments T5007
  • Workers' compensation benefits T5007
  • RRSP or RRIF withdrawal T4RSP, T4RIF
  • Support payments received
  • Sale of stocks, bonds or mutual funds (transaction records are required)
  • Sale of real estate (principal residence excluded)
  • Rental income
  • Business, farm or fishing income

What credit can be claimed?
  • RRSP contribution
  • Carrying charges and interest expenses paid to earn income from investments
  • Professional or union dues
  • Moving expenses
  • Child care expenses
  • Support for a child, spouse or common-law partner
  • Children's arts and sports programs
  • Adoption expenses
  • Tuition / education T2202 or T2202A
  • Interest paid on student loans
  • Medical expenses
  • Charitable Donations
  • Political contributions
  • First-time home buyer
  • Transit pass
  • Living with your parents or grandparents over 65
  • Rent or property tax paid in 2014
